 Beanjup Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Efe KIRBIZ, an independent software developer
based in Türkiye and publishing under the name Rapheldor Software ("Rapheldor,"
"we," "us," or "our"), handles information in connection with the mobile game
Beanjup (the "Game") for Android and iOS.

 1. Privacy at a Glance

The current version of Beanjup:

- does not require or offer user accounts;
- does not contain advertising;
- does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs;
- does not track users across apps or websites;
- does not collect precise or approximate location information;
- does not access contacts, photos, the camera, the microphone, or files;
- does not include in-app purchases;
- stores game preferences and high scores locally on your device; and
- allows you to voluntarily contact us through your own email application when
  reporting a bug or requesting support.

We do not sell personal information.

 2. Information Stored Only on Your Device

Beanjup stores a limited amount of information locally on your device so the
Game can remember your preferences:

- high scores and gameplay progress;
- selected language;
- music and sound settings;
- vibration settings; and
- whether you accepted the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

This information is not transmitted to Rapheldor. It normally remains on your
device until you clear the Game's app data or uninstall the Game. Device backup
and restore features provided by your operating system or app store may process
local app data in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

 3. Voluntary Support and Bug Reports

Beanjup includes a support screen that can open your device's email application.
The Game does not automatically send emails. You decide whether to send a
message and what information to include.

If you contact us by email, we may receive:

- your email address and any name associated with your email account;
- the subject and contents of your message;
- the Game version, operating system version, device specifications, and
  reproduction steps that you choose to provide; and
- screenshots, videos, or other attachments that you choose to include.

We use this information only to:

- respond to your request;
- investigate and fix bugs;
- improve the stability and usability of the Game;
- protect our support services from abuse; and
- comply with applicable legal obligations.

Please do not include passwords, government-issued identification numbers,
financial information, health information, precise location information, or any
other unnecessary sensitive personal information in your support message.

Our support email is hosted by a third-party email provider, currently Google's
Gmail service. Your email application and email provider also process your
message in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

 4. App Store and Platform Information

Google Play, the Apple App Store, Android, iOS, device manufacturers, and
operating system services may independently process information such as
downloads, installations, device details, app store account information, and
crash or performance diagnostics. Their processing of information is governed
by their own privacy policies and is not controlled by this Privacy Policy.

Rapheldor may receive aggregated or pseudonymized reports made available through
Google Play or the Apple App Store, such as the Game version, device model,
operating system version, crash stack traces, installation statistics, and
general performance information. We use these reports solely to maintain and
improve the Game and not for advertising or cross-app tracking.

 5. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

- Service providers: Providers that support our email, hosting, security, or
  technical operations, only to the extent necessary to provide those services.
- Legal requirements: When we reasonably believe disclosure is required by
  applicable law, a valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or
  security of users, Rapheldor, or others.
- Transfer of the Game: If ownership or operation of the Game is sold, assigned,
  reorganized, or otherwise transferred, information may be transferred subject
  to appropriate confidentiality and data protection safeguards.

We do not share personal information for behavioral advertising.

 6. Legal Grounds for Processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis for processing, we process personal
information:

- to respond to and fulfill requests you make, such as support requests;
- with your consent, where consent is required or appropriate;
- for our legitimate interests in securing, maintaining, debugging, and
  improving the Game, provided those interests do not override your rights and
  freedoms; and
- to comply with legal obligations or establish, exercise, or defend legal
  claims.

Support information is collected directly from you only when you voluntarily
send us an email. Gameplay information stored locally on your device is not
collected by Rapheldor.

 7. Data Retention and Deletion

- Local Game data remains on your device until you clear the Game's app data or
  uninstall the Game, subject to platform-controlled backup and restore
  features.
- Support messages are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond
  to your request, investigate issues, maintain support records, prevent abuse,
  and comply with legal obligations. We typically delete or anonymize support
  correspondence within 12 months after the related request has been resolved,
  unless a longer retention period is reasonably necessary for legal, security,
  or dispute-related purposes.
- Store diagnostics are retained according to the retention policies of Google
  Play and the Apple App Store.

Beanjup does not currently provide user accounts, so there is no Game account to
delete. You may request the deletion of a support email or other personal
information held by us by contacting rapheldorsoftware@gmail.com.

We may request sufficient information to locate and verify the relevant
correspondence before processing your request.

 8. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate to the
limited amount of information we handle. However, no email, device, storage
system, or method of electronic transmission is completely secure. You should
avoid sending unnecessary sensitive personal information by email.

 9. International Processing

Our service providers and app store providers may process information in
countries other than the country where you reside. Where required by law, we
rely on appropriate legal safeguards for international transfers. By
voluntarily contacting us, your message may be processed in Türkiye and in other
countries where your email provider or our email provider operates.

 10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- know whether we process your personal information;
- request access to or a copy of your personal information;
- request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information;
- request deletion or restriction of processing;
- object to certain types of processing;
- withdraw consent where processing is based on consent; and
- lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

These rights may be subject to legal limitations and exceptions.

To submit a privacy request, email rapheldorsoftware@gmail.com using the subject
line "Beanjup Privacy Request." We will respond in accordance with applicable
law.

Users in Türkiye may also have the rights provided under Article 11 of Turkish
Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK).

 11. Children and Teenagers

Beanjup is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. It is not directed
to children under the age of 13, and our Google Play target audience does not
include children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
Children under 13 should not use the Game or send us support messages. If you
are a parent or legal guardian and believe that a child under 13 has provided us
with personal information, please contact us at
rapheldorsoftware@gmail.com. We will take reasonable steps to locate and delete
that information.

Users aged 13 to 17 should ask a parent or legal guardian to review this Privacy
Policy and obtain any permission required under applicable law before sending
us personal information or attachments.

 12. Third-Party Links

The Game may open external links, including our website, this Privacy Policy,
the Terms of Use, app store pages, and your email application. We do not control
the privacy practices or content of third-party services. Please review their
privacy policies before providing any personal information.

 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the
Game, applicable laws, or our privacy practices. We will update the "Last
Updated" date and, where required, provide additional notice within the Game or
through the relevant app store.

If a future version of the Game introduces user accounts, advertising,
analytics, cloud saves, online leaderboards, in-app purchases, or other features
involving the processing of personal information, we will update this Privacy
Policy and the relevant app store privacy disclosures before or when those
features become available.
