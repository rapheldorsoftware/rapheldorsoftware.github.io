# Beanjup Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Efe KIRBIZ, an independent software developer
based in Türkiye and publishing under the name Rapheldor Software
("Rapheldor," "we," "us," or "our"), handles information in connection with
the mobile game Beanjup (the "Game") for Android and iOS.

## 1. Privacy at a Glance

The current version of Beanjup:

- does not require a separate Rapheldor account;
- uses Google Mobile Ads to provide interstitial and optional rewarded ads;
- uses Google's User Messaging Platform ("UMP") to manage advertising privacy
  choices where required;
- offers online leaderboards through Google Play Games on Android and Apple
  Game Center on iOS;
- stores gameplay progress, preferences, consent records, and pending score
  uploads locally on your device;
- does not provide chat or allow users to type a public name inside the Game;
- does not include in-app purchases;
- does not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or files; and
- allows you to voluntarily contact us through your own email application for
  support or bug reports.

We do not sell personal information for money. Advertising and platform
providers may process data as described below and under their own privacy
policies.

## 2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Beanjup stores a limited amount of information locally so the Game can remember
its state and your choices, including:

- local high scores and gameplay or tutorial progress;
- selected language;
- music, sound, and vibration settings;
- whether you accepted the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use;
- advertising consent or privacy-choice records stored by the UMP SDK; and
- scores waiting to be submitted to Google Play Games or Game Center, together
  with limited synchronization state.

Most of this information remains on your device. If you are signed in to the
applicable gaming service, leaderboard scores are transmitted to Google Play
Games or Apple Game Center as described in Section 4.

Local data normally remains until you clear the Game's app data or uninstall
the Game. Device backup and restore features supplied by your operating system
or app store may process local app data under their own terms and policies.

## 3. Advertising and Advertising Privacy Choices

Beanjup integrates the Google Mobile Ads SDK and Google's UMP SDK. The Game may
show:

- interstitial ads at selected transitions; and
- optional rewarded ads that you may choose to watch for one gameplay
  continuation.

Depending on your region, consent choices, device settings, platform
permissions, and Google's configuration, Google Mobile Ads and participating
advertising partners may automatically collect or receive information such as:

- IP address, which may be used to estimate a general location such as country
  or region;
- device or account identifiers, including the Android advertising ID, app set
  ID, Apple's advertising identifier (IDFA) where available and permitted, or
  other app- or developer-scoped identifiers;
- app and advertising interactions, such as app launches, taps, ads displayed,
  ad clicks, and video views;
- advertising data and ad performance information;
- device, operating system, Game version, and language information;
- crash, diagnostic, performance, and SDK reliability information; and
- consent signals and privacy choices needed to determine which ads may be
  requested.

Google and advertising partners may use this information to provide,
personalize, limit, select, deliver, and measure ads; detect fraud and abuse;
maintain security; provide analytics related to advertising; and improve their
services. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized/contextual depending on
your choices and applicable law. Choosing non-personalized ads does not mean
that no data is processed; limited data may still be used for ad delivery,
frequency capping, measurement, security, and fraud prevention.

Where required, Beanjup asks the UMP SDK to display a privacy message before
requesting ads. If a privacy-options entry point is required for your region,
it is available from the Game's settings. You may also control or reset
advertising identifiers and tracking permissions in your device settings.

For more information, review the
[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and
[Google's advertising technologies information](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 4. Google Play Games and Apple Game Center Leaderboards

Beanjup offers weekly and all-time leaderboards through:

- Google Play Games on Android; and
- Apple Game Center on iOS.

These features use the gaming profile managed by your platform provider. The
Game does not create a separate Rapheldor account and does not receive your
Google or Apple password.

When you are signed in to the applicable service, the Game and platform may
process:

- your platform gamer name, nickname, or display name;
- a platform-scoped player identifier;
- your submitted Beanjup score;
- your leaderboard rank and leaderboard period;
- authentication and score-submission status; and
- related game activity, device, security, and diagnostic information handled
  by the platform provider.

The Game submits completed-run scores to the applicable weekly and all-time
leaderboards. The platform service keeps the best qualifying score according to
the leaderboard configuration. Beanjup receives leaderboard entries so it can
display gamer names, scores, and ranks in the Game's custom leaderboard screen.
This information may therefore be visible to other players according to your
Google Play Games or Game Center profile and privacy settings.

If a score cannot be uploaded immediately, Beanjup may retain the score locally
and retry later. We do not operate a separate leaderboard server or retain a
separate copy of the complete leaderboard outside the platform service.

You can manage profile visibility, sign-in, and game data through the relevant
platform settings. More information is available from
[Google Play Games profile privacy settings](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/3129346)
and [Apple's Game Center & Privacy notice](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/data/en/game-center/).

## 5. Voluntary Support and Bug Reports

Beanjup includes a support screen that can open your device's email application.
The Game does not automatically send an email. You decide whether to send a
message and what information to include.

If you contact us, we may receive:

- your email address and any name associated with your email account;
- the subject and contents of your message;
- Game version, operating system version, device specifications, and
  reproduction steps that you choose to provide; and
- screenshots, videos, or other attachments that you choose to include.

We use this information to respond, investigate and fix issues, improve the
Game, protect support channels from abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Please do not send passwords, government identifiers, financial information,
health information, precise location, or other unnecessary sensitive personal
information. Our support email is currently hosted by Google's Gmail service;
your own email provider also processes the message under its policies.

## 6. App Stores, Devices, and Diagnostics

Google Play, the Apple App Store, Android, iOS, device manufacturers, and
operating-system services may independently process downloads, installations,
device details, store-account information, game-service activity, and crash or
performance diagnostics under their own policies.

Rapheldor may receive aggregated or pseudonymized store reports such as Game
version, device model, operating-system version, crash stack traces,
installation statistics, advertising performance, and general performance
information. We use available reports to operate, secure, debug, and improve
the Game and its monetization.

Beanjup does not currently integrate Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics,
or a separate Rapheldor behavioral-analytics service. This does not limit the
advertising, measurement, diagnostics, or platform processing described in
this Policy.

## 7. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell or rent personal information for money.

Information may be processed or disclosed in these limited circumstances:

- **Advertising providers:** Google Mobile Ads and participating advertising
  partners, for ad delivery, personalization where permitted, measurement,
  analytics, security, and fraud prevention.
- **Gaming platforms:** Google Play Games and Apple Game Center, to authenticate
  players and provide scores, ranks, profiles, and leaderboards.
- **Operational providers:** Providers supporting email, website hosting,
  security, app distribution, or technical operations, only as necessary.
- **Legal and safety reasons:** When reasonably required by applicable law,
  valid legal process, or to protect users, Rapheldor, or others.
- **Business transfer:** If ownership or operation of the Game is transferred,
  subject to appropriate confidentiality and data-protection safeguards.

Some privacy laws use terms such as "sale," "sharing," or "targeted
advertising" more broadly than an exchange for money. Where required, you may
exercise relevant advertising choices through the UMP privacy-options screen or
your device settings.

## 8. Purposes and Legal Grounds

We and our providers process information, as applicable:

- to provide gameplay, ads, rewarded continuations, sign-in, and leaderboards;
- to remember choices and synchronize scores;
- to respond to support requests;
- to measure advertising and Game performance;
- to detect fraud, cheating, abuse, and security incidents;
- with consent where consent is required;
- for legitimate interests in operating, securing, debugging, and improving the
  Game, where those interests do not override your rights; and
- to comply with legal obligations and establish, exercise, or defend claims.

## 9. Retention and Deletion

- Local Game data remains on your device until cleared or the Game is
  uninstalled, subject to platform backup and restore behavior.
- Pending leaderboard scores are removed from local pending storage after a
  successful upload or when an expired weekly pending score is no longer
  applicable.
- Google and Apple retain gaming profile, score, advertising, and diagnostic
  data according to their own policies and your platform settings.
- Support correspondence is normally deleted or anonymized within 12 months
  after the request is resolved, unless longer retention is reasonably needed
  for legal, security, abuse-prevention, or dispute purposes.
- Store and advertising reports are retained according to the relevant
  provider's policies and our reasonable operational needs.

Beanjup has no separate Rapheldor game account to delete. You can manage or
delete Google Play Games data through Google Play Games settings and manage
Game Center through Apple device/account settings. You may contact us for help
identifying the appropriate control or to request deletion of support
information held directly by Rapheldor.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- request deletion or restriction;

## 13. Children and Teenagers

Beanjup is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. It is not directed
to children under 13, and our Google Play target audience does not include
children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under
13. Children under 13 should not use the Game or send support messages. If you
believe a child under 13 has provided personal information directly to us,
contact rapheldorsoftware@gmail.com so we can take reasonable steps to locate
and delete it.

Users aged 13 to 17 should ask a parent or legal guardian to review this Policy
and obtain any permission required by applicable law. Platform providers may
apply additional protections, restrictions, or consent requirements to minors
and supervised accounts.
